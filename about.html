The Story

Why I Built This

Virtual Labor Force was built on a simple observation: AI is transforming how the world works — but most small and mid-sized businesses in Detroit and across America have no practical path to using it.

The tools exist. The technology is accessible. What is missing is someone willing to bridge the gap — to translate AI capability into practical business value, without the enterprise price tag or the consultant jargon.

That is what Virtual Labor Force does.

The Truth Preservation Network emerged from a deeper belief — that AI without verified truth is dangerous, and that humanity has an obligation to preserve its memory before it disappears. TPN is not just a product. It is a mission.
